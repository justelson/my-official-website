FAQ

Is the app safe to use?

Yes, absolutely! All our apps are designed with security as a top priority. We don't collect or store your personal files - everything happens locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers, and we use industry-standard encryption to protect any settings or preferences you save.

Is the YouTube channel worth subscribing to?

Yes! Our YouTube channel is regularly updated with tutorials, productivity tips, and advanced features of our apps. Subscribers get early access to new videos and can influence future content through polls and comments. It's a great resource for maximizing your productivity journey.

How do I get started with the File Organizer?

Getting started is simple! After downloading, just run the app and follow the intuitive setup wizard. You'll select which folders you want to organize, choose your preferred organization rules, and the app does the rest automatically. There's a comprehensive tutorial included, and you can always adjust settings later as needed.

Will the app delete any of my files?

No, the app will never delete your files without explicit permission. By default, it only organizes files by moving them to appropriate folders. There is an optional duplicate detection feature, but even then, files are only flagged for your review - you always maintain control over what happens to your files.

Which app version is right for me?

If you're primarily organizing personal documents, photos, and downloads, the Personal version is perfect. For business documents and collaborative environments, choose the Office version. Content creators working with large media libraries should select the Media version for specialized tools for video, audio, and image files.
